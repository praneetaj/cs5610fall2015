Welcome!

LoyalU is a web application that solves coupon management and aggregation for 2 types of users:
  1. Restaurant Business Owners:
    Many food outlets owners have a way to track and reward loyal customers. This can be in the form of paper cards that are punched when a customer purchases any items or a magnetic card that stores the customer's sales history. These solutions can be expensive and difficult to manage. Loyal coupons allows restaurant owners to:
    • Release, manage and redeem loyalty based coupons to their customers.
    • Release promotional coupons that are not loyalty based.
  2. Food Consumers (Users):
    A typical user today maintains different physical cards to maintain their customer loyalty status and redeem benefits. Moreover, to get deals for various food outlets they have to access multiple websites. Loyal coupons allows users to:
    • Keep track of all their customer loyalty activity electronically with a single account, thereby eliminating the need to carry multiple physical cards everywhere.
    • Look for food outlets nearby and get access to other deals and promotional coupons that will be sourced from external websites like Groupon, Living social, Amazon local deals etc. so that they don't have to look elsewhere.
